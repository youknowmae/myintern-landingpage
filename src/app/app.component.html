About Us!

MyIntern is a platform designed specifically to help students of Gordon College's College of Computer Studies (CCS) in finding the right On-the-Job Training (OJT) opportunities. It streamlines the entire process, making it easier for students to search for internships, apply directly through the platform, and track the progress of their applications. In addition to this, MyIntern offers various tools and features that help students stay organized and informed, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience from start to finish.
