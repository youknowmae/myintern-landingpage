About Us!

ITrack is a comprehensive platform tailored to meet the needs of Gordon College's College of Computer Studies (CCS) students in their quest for On-the-Job Training (OJT) opportunities. Our mission is to simplify the internship process by providing a seamless system that allows students to explore, apply for, and monitor their OJT applications in one place.

With user-friendly tools and features, ITrack ensures that students can stay organized, informed, and confident throughout their internship journey. By streamlining every step, from finding opportunities to tracking progress, we aim to enhance the overall experience, helping students focus on building their careers.

Our team of passionate developers and an experienced adviser are dedicated to making ITrack an indispensable resource for students, fostering growth and success for the future professionals of CCS.

ITrack.
